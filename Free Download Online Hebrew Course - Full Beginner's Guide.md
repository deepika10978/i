## Common Hebrew Phrases to Get You Started

Here are a few essential Hebrew phrases to get you started on your language learning journey:

*   **Shalom (שלום):** Hello / Goodbye / Peace
*   **Ma Shlomcha? (מה שלומך?):** How are you? (to a male)
*   **Ma Shlomech? (מה שלומך?):** How are you? (to a female)
*   **Tov Me'od (טוב מאוד):** Very good
*   **Todah (תודה):** Thank you
*   **Bevakasha (בבקשה):** You're welcome / Please
*   **Ken (כן):** Yes
*   **Lo (לא):** No
*   **Shmi... (שמי...):** My name is...
*   **Eifo...? (איפה...?):** Where is...?
